What We Do

What exactly does "Mission Vigilant" do? We help raise money through various events such as 5k walk/run, raffle tickets, and more. With the funds we receive, we provide support to veterans and first responders by covering their therapy expenses.

Who We Help

We support veterans who struggle with mental health issues due to their service experiences. Additionally, we assist first responders including police officers, firefighters, military personnel, and paramedics.

Why This Journey Was Started

My name is Conner Cleland, and I want you to understand the pain of losing a loved one to suicide. When I was 14, I woke up to officers informing me of my brother's suicide. The pain hit me hard, and I don't want anyone else to experience it. With this charity, I aim to prevent such tragedies. Please donate to help us support those in need.
